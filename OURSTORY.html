Our Story!

Where you can find us: EATING

We are four food enthusiasts from London. We love to cook and go out and try new places in London!

We started our food journey as four hungry students of Imperial College London. After a year of eating low quality uni food, we decided that it was time to explore London and take advantages of the incredible food that this city has to offer!

Ultimately, we are just four girls who love a good meal - join us on our quest for the most delectable food in this city!
